Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our penetration testing services

How long does a typical penetration test take?

Depending on the scope and complexity, a typical penetration test can take anywhere from 1-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

What's included in the final report?

Our reports include detailed findings, risk assessments, proof-of-concept demonstrations, and actionable remediation recommendations with step-by-step guidance.

Do you provide ongoing support after the test?

Yes, we provide post-testing support including remediation guidance, follow-up testing, and ongoing security consultation to ensure your systems remain secure.

Are your tests compliant with industry standards?

Absolutely. We follow OWASP, NIST, and CREST methodologies to ensure our testing meets industry standards and regulatory requirements.

What kind of companies do you work with?

I work with startups, SMEs, and large enterprises from various industries. Every project is tailored to your needs.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

I take confidentiality very seriously. All client data and findings are handled with strict privacy and professionalism.

How quickly can you start a project?

I can usually start within a few days after our initial consultation and agreement.

Do you provide reports in English?

Yes, all reports and communication can be provided in English.

Can you help after the test is done?

Yes, I provide support for explaining the findings and, if needed, performing additional verification tests. I offer recommendations to improve your security, but implementation and ongoing monitoring are the client’s responsibility.
